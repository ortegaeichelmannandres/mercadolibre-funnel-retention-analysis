# 🛒 MercadoLibre: Conversion Funnel & User Retention Analysis

## Project Overview
This analysis tracks the user journey from landing on a product page to completing a purchase. The goal was to identify where users drop off and how "sticky" the platform is across different LATAM regions.

## 📈 Executive Summary
- **The Bottleneck:** The single largest point of friction is the `add_to_cart` stage, with an average drop-off of **65.49%**. 
- **Regional Insight:** **Peru** shows the highest friction at the cart stage (74.5% drop), while **Uruguay** is the most efficient (59.9%).
- **Retention Trends:** Retention remains stable during the first three weeks but sees a significant dip at **Day 28**, particularly in Argentina and Colombia.

## 🛠️ Methodology
- **Funnel Analysis:** Mapping the 6 stages of the purchase journey.
- **Cohort Analysis:** Tracking user behavior over 4-week periods (D7, D14, D21, D28).
- **Segmentation:** Comparing performance across 10 LATAM countries.

## 💡 Recommendations
1. **Cart Optimization:** Implement A/B testing on the "Add to Cart" button/UI to reduce the 65% drop-off.
2. **D28 Reactivation:** Launch push notifications or targeted discounts at Day 25 to improve 4-week retention.
